My Projects

Two Sentence Horror Gallery

Users can log in and post their own two sentence horror stories.
They can also rate and comment other users posts.
Anyone who visits the site can filter through latest, oldest and highest rated posts.
The authentication was built using Laravel Breezes built in authentication package.
This is my first backend project using a framework. Laravel was easy to use and well documented.

Built with:
Laravel PHP Breeze TailwindCSS MySQL Render

PokeStore Direct

Users can search for any pokemon product by filtering or searching and add it to their basket
The basket calculates their total amount and shows their order, if they follow it through they are greeted to a little easter egg.
This was my first time working with an API and found it challenging yet rewarding.
